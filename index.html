  • Bio

    • Ompa is a young minded Graphics and Web enthusiast. He also enjoys editing for film and music videos. He thinks of himself as a creative multimedia artist.
    Ompa comes from the beautiful Chimbu Province situated in the heart of Papua New Guinea. He arrived in New Zealand in 1999 on an academic scholarship. He has played representative and international Rugby League whilst living in N.Z.

    Read more

    He has been a single father to his only son (Nelann) since 2008 after a possible professional League career deemed unlikely.
    He then went back to study in 2010 and in 2014 he graduated with a Bachelor of Creative Technology with a major in Digital Media. In 2016 he enrolled at Yoobee School of Design to learn to code. He hopes to get real experience in the creative industry where design and code can become one.
    Today, Omphalus is still a single-dad and works as a freelance digital designer from home.
