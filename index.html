Hello and Welcome,

My name is Omphalus Harkie Kua but all my friends call me Ompa. I come from the beautiful Chimbu Province which is located within the Highlands region of Papua New Guinea. I enjoy Graphic Design, Web development, Photography, Film & Motion graphics and music.
This is my online Portfolio which is a working progress and will constantly grow/change as I populate it with more contents. At this stage it is at it's foundation. I hope you will enjoy this small experience.

Read more

My graphic design skills halped me build or atleast design great frontend User Interface with wirefreframes and mockups in illustrator. Yet, with no prior exposure to any sort of coding in html5 and CSS or the javascript programming language, I was instantly petrified when I first saw what code looked like on a black screen. However, using all these elements to allow my design skills make a page come alive was really satisfying. After making a few failed attempts at certain school projects, in terms of development in the end the nuts and bolts of website development was acquired through some self perserverence in repetition and by following Youtube tutorials. I am still learning a whole bunch and I wish to expand further and become a great progrmmer for Apps one day. This Digital Portfolio you are currently looking at is a result of a self-thought project.

GRAPHIC DESIGN

What have I discovered?

I feel a lot more confident with graphic design in the whole scheme of Creativity. I have to say firstly that with Graphic design, I have found a real motive or should I say, it has developed into a passion which I see no bounds to. I like to create or amplify a design with a finesse.

Read more

I like to think that any design is a 'navigational path' or a way of moving ones perspective. Therefore a good design allows for a person or persons to interact with it. It certainly serves as a means of relaying a message or force an action or thought upon someone. With Graphic Design, it is mainly visual. Therefore Graphic design creates a visual emotion. Basically if you can see it, you are connected.
Design elements such as Typography, Shapes, Colors, Texts, Images, Size and composition can alter one's emotions just like how you would feel while listenning to music. They are all very important yet I find composition to be the most detrimental factor in graphic design because spacing is important. Every other element will have to compensate each other for an available space.
Sound and motion are elements of Digital media that bring or add a dynamic output to any static picture to bring life into the scene. I hope that what I say here is good enough to be warranted as good facts on Graphic Design by someone out there as well.

Tyography

Using different typefaces helps with my design to focus the theme to a certain genre, whether it be retro, contemporary or a particular theme of the design. It is an important tool to use in graphic design where I use to improve the final outlook of a design. My two favourite typefaces are source-sans pro and open sans.

About

About this portfolio
This portfolio was created to showcase what I have done and prove my potential in this field of creativity. In a way I can get to appreciate and learn about myself with much more ease in terms of excess.
The code for this portfolio does not include any Frame-works like Bootstrap me to have a better understanding of the relationships between Html, Css and javascript plus Jquery. I plan on using one of the major framewo-works of either Bootstrap, Foundation and or Wordpress to put my portfolio on once I finish this one. Writing my own classes and ID's allows me to be more tidier and smarter in my next projects. I have used gulp to precompile and minify the sass, script.js and images files. I have also run autoprefixer to handle the Flex-box items. I have tried to make the site Responsive across mobile, tablet and desktop. I cretainly enjoyed coding my own portfolio. It has taken a while but I am glad that Github keeps a history of my constant change.


Little about Me
Ompa is a young minded Web and Graphic Designer and Frontend Web-Developer. He also enjoys editing for film or music videos. He thinks of myself as a multimedia artist. At this point, he hopes to start a professional career through design and coding to earn his experience.

He has a Bachelor in Creative Technology with a major in Digital Media at the end of 2014. He then went on to learn coding to become a frontend web-Developer in 2016. His current goal is to get some solid years of experience in this creative industry where design and coding can become one. He hopes that this portfolio web site can help me in acheiveing my goals.

Ompa has a good a good understanding of the Adobe Creative suite. He is great with Photoshop and Illustrator and he enjoys Premier Pro for editing film and he likes doing small motion graphics with After-effects. He recently took coding for websites, including HTML5, javascript/jquery, css/sass and he is familiar the bootstrap and foundation frameworks as well as WordPress for backened projects. He ejoys his talent in making music/beats and writing songs.

He wishes to have his own creative digital studio business in the future but as for now, he is happy to learn and expand on my skills and gain real experience in the industry.

As an artist, he thanks you for visiting and he hope you have had a pleasent experience through this website so far. Do feel free to contact him.
