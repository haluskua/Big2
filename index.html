About this- App
Web Dev Setup
This web app has allowed me to appreciate the development aspect of the project itself. I have used gulp to precompile and minify the sass, script.js, and images files and HTML. I have also run autoprefixer to handle the Flex-box items. I have tried to make the site Responsive across mobile, tablet and desktop . Most of the device testing has been done on my phone and using Chrome's inspector tool for device testing. There are minor bugs on a mobile device in Landscape view mainly with the drop-down menu not showing when alternating between different views from Portrait to landscape.
As for browser compatibility, only Google Chrome browser testing have been done.
About -Ompa
Omphalus Harkie Kua
Ompa is a young minded Graphics and Web enthusiast. He also enjoys editing for film and music videos. He thinks of himself as a creative multimedia artist.
Ompa comes from the beautiful Chimbu Province situated in the heart of Papua New Guinea. He arrived in New Zealand in 1999 on an academic scholarship. He has played representative and international Rugby League whilst living in N.Z. He has been a single father to his only son (Nelann) since 2008, after a possible proffessional League career deemed unlikely.
He then went back to studying in 2010 and in 2014 he graduated with a Bachelor of Creative Technology with a major in Digital Media. In 2016 he enrolled at Yoobee School of Design to learn coding. He hopes to get real experience in the creative industry where design and code can become one.

Today, Omphalus is still a single-dad and works as a freelance digital designer from home.
