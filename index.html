Web

Where am I with that?

I am driving momentum towards web design and web development mainly in the front end. This is mainly due to fact that I already have a sound Graphic Design skill. So transferring it to more dynamic platforms such as web is quite exciting and more satisfying in terms of enjoyment.

Read more

I took a full year course at Yoobee school do design for Web development in 2016. There, my graphic design skills helped me build or atleast design great frontend User Interface with wirefreframes and mockups in illustrator. Yet, with no prior exposure to any sort of coding in html5 and CSS or the javascript programming language, I was insanely petrified when I first saw what code looked like on a black computer screen. However, making a simple Html page come alive was really satisfying. After making a few failed attempts at certain school projects, my passion grew and after following Youtube tutorials, I was able to grasp the relationship between Html, Csss, and Javascrip/jQuery. I am still learning a whole bunch and I wish to expand further and become a great progrmmer for Apps one day - or even a software engineer. This Digital Portfolio you are currently looking at is a result of a self-thought project.

Development Setup

I use either Sublime text and or Atom as my main IDE. I am familiar with HTML5 and CSS3 with basic Javascript and jQuery. I have been leaning the sass version of css as I find the code a little bit easier to read which I believe is mostly to do with the fact that I am a designer. I use gitbash to upload all my "commits" from my local computer upto github "haluskua". I totally enjoy using Flexbox. I believe the two top frameworks which are Bootstrap and Foundation have incorparated the Flexbox classes into their setup which is great. I am familiar with both frameworks but have yet to finish a whole project so it is certainly my next goal.

What I've Learned

I feel a lot more confident with graphic design in the whole scheme of Creativity. I have to say firstly that with Graphic design, I have found a real motive or should I say, a passion which I see no bounds to. I like to create or amplify a design with a finesse. I could be somewhat a perfectionist which is something I try not be be too much as it does take a long time to acheive.

Read more

I like to think that any design is a 'navigational path' to or for ones perspective. Therefore a good design allows for a person or persons to connect with it gracefully. It certainly serves as a means of relaying a message or force an action. feeling or thought upon the audience as Graphic Design is mainly visual. Therefore Graphic design creates a visual emotion. Basically if you can see it, you are connected.
Design elements such as Typography, Shapes, Colors, Texts, Images, Size and composition can alter one's emotions just like how you would feel while listenning to music. They are all very important yet I find composition to be the most detrimental factor in graphic design because spacing is important because composition brings all together as one like a music track.
Sound and motion are elements of Digital media that bring or add a dynamic output to any static picture to bring life into the scene. I hope that what I say here is good enough to be warranted as good facts for Graphic Design by others out there.

Tyography

Using different typefaces helps with my design to focus the theme to a certain genre, whether it be retro, contemporary or a particular theme of the design. It is an important tool to use in graphic design where I use it to improve the final outlook of a design. My two favourite typefaces are Helvetica and Open sans.

About
My portfolio

This portfolio was created to showcase what I have done and show my potential in the field of creativity.
The code for this portfolio does not include any Frame-works like Bootstrap to let learn and better understand coding with between Html5, Css and javascript plus Jquery syntex. I plan on using one of the major framewo-works of either Bootstrap, Foundation and or Wordpress later to put my portfolio on once I finish this one. Writing my own classes and ID's will allow me to be more tidier and smarter in my next projects.

I have used gulp to precompile and minify the sass, script.js and images files and html. I have also run autoprefixer to handle the Flex-box items. I have tried to make the site Responsive across mobile, tablet and desktop. It has taken a while but I am glad that Github keeps a history of my constant change.


Little about Me
Omphalus H. Kua comes from the beautiful Chimbu Province situated right in the middle of Papua New Guinea. He arrived in New Zealand in 1999 on an academic scholarship. He has since learned to appreciate a wonderful life here in beautiful Aotearoa N.Z.
The most endearing struggle he has had to face yet while living here was becoming a single father to his only son in 2008 while persuing his dream of becoming an art director. Today he is still a single dad but wiser. He has appreciated every moment since with his son Nelann who has just turned 14 this year. His only wish is to get him through to University so he may also acheive his dream - whatever that may be for the young man.

Ompa is a young minded Web and Graphic Designer and Frontend Web-Developer. He also enjoys editing for film or music videos. He thinks of himself as a multimedia artist. Ompa wishes to secure a positive career in the Creative industry to give his son and him a good future. This is his first online portfolio of work.

He has a Bachelor in Creative Technology with a major in Digital Media at the end of 2014. He then went on to learn coding to become a frontend web-Developer in 2016. His current goal is to get some solid years of experience in this creative industry where design and coding can become one.

Core Skills

Ompa has a good understanding of the Adobe Creative suite. He is great with Photoshop, Illustrator, Indesign and he enjoys Premier Pro for editing film and he likes doing small motion graphics projects with After-effects. He recently took coding for websites, including HTML5, javascript/jquery, css/sass and he is familiar the bootstrap and foundation frameworks as well as WordPress for backened projects. He also ejoys his talent in making music/beats and writing songs. He is can touch type and has had much exposure with mircosoft programs.
